Galileo Galilei

He was the first space person with a telescope. Others looked at stars, but Galileo was the first to really study them with a telescope he made by himself.

The moon has impurities. He saw the moon wasn't a smooth ball. It was all bumpy with mountains and holes, just like Earth!

He found Jupiter's moons. He saw four little dots next to Jupiter and realized they were its own private moons going around it. Not everything circles us!

Venus has a light switch. Galileo saw Venus change shapes, like our moon. It went from a tiny sliver to a full circle, which proved it was going around the sun.

The Milky Way is a pile of stars. He saw it was actually many stars squished together.

Galileo saw dark spots on the sun (sunspots) and watched them move.

He was curious and open minded. He drew everything he saw in a notebook. He was like a detective collecting clues about space.

He stood up to peer pressure and followed his own beliefs in astronomy. He believed the sun was the center of the solar system, not Earth. This got him in big trouble.

He was a true mathematician. He used math to figure out how far away stuff was and how it moved. He said the universe was a math problem to solve.

He was eliminated. The people at the top said he was wrong and put him under house arrest for the rest of his life for ideas and hypotheses on astronomy.
